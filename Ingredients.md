# Ingredients

## <u>  FILLING  </u>

* 16 oz (2 pack) full fat cream cheese, room temperature

* 1 C powdered sugar

* 1 tsp lemon juice, fresh

* 1 tsp lemon zest

* 1 tsp vanilla essence

* 1 1/2C heavy cream, cold


## <u> CRUST  </u>
* 1 1/2 C graham cracker crumbs

* 3 tbsp granulated sugar

* 6 tbsp melted butter, unsalted
